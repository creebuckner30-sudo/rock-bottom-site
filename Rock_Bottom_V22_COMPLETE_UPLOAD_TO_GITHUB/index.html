Filmmaker Statement

Cree Kawa and Amalya Barseghyan are young actors who created Rock Bottom to showcase their acting and writing abilities.

Putting art first, they set out to tell a tender, twisted, and deeply relevant queer story centered on connection, vulnerability, and the complicated relationships we build with one another.

Created independently and driven by a passion for authentic storytelling, Rock Bottom represents a new generation of filmmakers committed to character-driven cinema that explores identity, intimacy, and belonging.

The Story

Loyalty is tested.

The past comes back to haunt the protagonists, unearthing secrets and old feelings. It becomes a journey to Rock Bottom.

Visual Language

Warm, toxic, tender.

The lookbook builds a world of California sunset tones, shadowed interiors, warm reds, amber practicals, and intimate close-ups.
